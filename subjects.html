Athilesh prefers to approach tutoring from a relationship-based perspective. While pre-exam cram sessions are available, he highly recommends scheduling a couple of sessions together to get to know a student's notetaking habits, patterns of mistakes, etc. This helps to not only bridge gaps in material by tailoring content delivery to the individual but also improve students' study and scheduling habits.
